About me

Hi, I'm Matheus Fagundes and I'm a PhD candidate working under the supervision of Dr. Woodson at the University of Georgia.

My Phd dissertation lies at the intersaction of nearshore dynamics and turbulence due to vegetation and how these interactions affect larvae dispersal and nutrients.

My interests range from high-resolution idealized modeling to Global Climate modeling and more recently ML/AI.
