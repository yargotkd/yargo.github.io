About me

Hi, I'm Yargo Teixeira Gomes de Melo and I'm a PhD candidate working under the supervision of Dr. Woodson at the University of Georgia.

My dissertaion is on Langmuir Supercells, an important mechanism of resuspension and transport of sediments in the coastal ocean, which may greatly affect the local benthic ecosystem.

My interests are in fluid dynamics ranging from physical oceanography to fluid-body interactions, and more recently high-resolution idealized modeling to global climate and ocean modeling.
